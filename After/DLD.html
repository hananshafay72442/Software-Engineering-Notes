IC Quick Reference
Basic Gates
74LS00
Quad 2-input NAND
4 independent NAND gates. Fundamental universal gate — can implement any logic function.
74LS02
Quad 2-input NOR
4 independent NOR gates. Also universal — used in SR latches and basic memory cells.
74LS04
Hex Inverter (NOT)
6 independent NOT gates. Used everywhere — complement generation, enable inversion.
74LS08
Quad 2-input AND
4 independent AND gates. Used in decoder circuits and carry generation.
74LS32
Quad 2-input OR
4 independent OR gates. Used to sum minterms in decoder-based function implementation.
74LS86
Quad 2-input XOR
4 XOR gates. Core of half adder (Sum) and full adder circuits.
Adders & Arithmetic (Topic 6A)
74x283
4-bit Binary Full Adder
Adds two 4-bit numbers + carry-in. Uses carry-lookahead internally for speed. Chainable for 8/16-bit addition.
74x85
4-bit Magnitude Comparator
Outputs A=B, A>B, A<B. Has cascading inputs — chain chips for wider comparisons. Init LSB chip with A=B_IN=1.
74x682
8-bit Magnitude Comparator
Compares two 8-bit words. Outputs P=Q and P>Q (active-low). Used in address decoders and control logic.
Decoders & Encoders (Topic 6B)
74138
3-to-8 Line Decoder
Active-LOW outputs. Three enable pins (G1 active-HIGH, G2A & G2B active-LOW — all must be satisfied). Classic for memory address decoding.
74139
Dual 2-to-4 Line Decoder
Two independent 2-to-4 decoders with active-LOW enable and outputs. Compact for small address spaces.
74148
8-to-3 Priority Encoder
Priority encoder — if multiple inputs active, highest-priority (highest index) wins. Active-LOW inputs and outputs.
Multiplexers & Demux (Topic 6C)
74151A
8-to-1 Multiplexer
Selects 1 of 8 data inputs. Has both Y (normal) and W (complemented) outputs. Active-LOW enable. Standard for Boolean function implementation.
74153
Dual 4-to-1 Multiplexer
Two independent 4-to-1 MUXes sharing common select lines. Active-LOW enable per section.
74157
Quad 2-to-1 Multiplexer
Four 2-to-1 MUXes with shared select & enable. Used to steer 4-bit data buses between two sources.
Flip-Flops & Latches (Topic 7)
7474
Dual D Flip-Flop (Positive Edge)
Two D flip-flops with PRE and CLR asynchronous inputs. Rising-edge triggered. The workhorse register element.
74112
Dual JK Flip-Flop (Negative Edge)
Two JK flip-flops, falling-edge triggered. With J=K=1: toggles. Used in ripple counters.
Counters (Topic 8)
74163
Synchronous 4-bit Binary Counter
Counts 0–15. Synchronous clear & load. Terminal Count (TC) output for cascading. The standard synchronous counter chip.
74193
Synchronous Up/Down Counter
4-bit bidirectional counter with separate UP and DOWN clock inputs. Borrow/carry outputs for cascading.
74190
Synchronous BCD Up/Down Counter
Decade (MOD-10) counter. Counts 0–9. Ideal for decimal display systems and frequency dividers.
